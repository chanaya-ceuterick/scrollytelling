MY STORY OF LIVING WITH ANXIETY

Step into my world

Anxiety is more than just worry or stress—it’s an invisible force that grips your mind and body, often without warning. It’s a constant battle, where even the smallest tasks can feel overwhelming.

For me, it’s like a relentless pulse, a weight pressing on my chest, and a storm raging in my thoughts. It’s not just something I feel—it’s something I live with.

As you scroll through this page, I invite you to step into my experience. Feel the pulsing, the pressure, the chaos, and the moments of calm that follow. This is what anxiety feels like to me.
